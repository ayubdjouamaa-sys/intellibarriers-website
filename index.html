Meet the IntelliBarrier unit

Designed as heavy infrastructure — not a gadget. A self-powered, modular barrier that can form rigid walls, create queues, and reconfigure layouts without manual labor.

Omnidirectional mobility

Omni-wheels enable smooth lateral movement (strafing) for precise positioning in dense environments.

  • Sideways alignment without turning
  • Controlled movement profiles for safety
  • Obstacle avoidance & positioning sensors

AI-powered crowd analysis

High-quality cameras and AI models analyze pedestrian flow and density to flag risk patterns early.

  • Detect bottlenecks and overcrowding
  • Predict flow direction and surge risk
  • Operator dashboard with alerts

IB-Lock™ interlocking system

Motor-assisted mechanical coupling that locks units into rigid wall formations, designed to be fail-safe.

  • Male/female side coupling for fast alignment
  • No power required to stay locked
  • Manual override for emergencies
